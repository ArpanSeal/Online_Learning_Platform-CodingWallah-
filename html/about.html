We Started CodingWallah in 2023.

Coding Wallah is an EdTech startup founded in 2023 by Arpan Seal. It is an e-learning platform that offers quality learning experiences at an affordable cost. This startup provides lectures and solutions on various Computer Science subjects for students & Professionals. Coding Wallah presents both live and scheduled lectures along with doubt-solving sessions, and structured materials and helps students evaluate themselves through tests.
